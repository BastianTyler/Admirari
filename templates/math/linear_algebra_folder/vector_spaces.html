Intro To Vector Spaces

What is a Vector Space

Definition: Let V be a non-empty set. We will call the elements of V vectors and defined on V is an addition operation and a scalar multiplication operation with scalar in some set F, where F could be the real numbers, integers, or whatever. We can call V a vector space if it satisfies ten conditions. We will go over those in a moment.
