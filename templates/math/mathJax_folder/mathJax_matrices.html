LaTeX Code for Matrices and Systems of Equations

This page shows the LaTeX code for creating matrices and systems of equations, followed by the actual output rendered using MathJax.

1. LaTeX Code for Matrices
1.1 Matrix with Square Brackets (\begin{bmatrix} ... \end{bmatrix})

LaTeX Code:

\[
A = \begin{bmatrix} 
    1 & 2 & 3 \\
    4 & 5 & 6 \\
    7 & 8 & 9 
\end{bmatrix}
\]

Rendered Output:

$$ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \end{bmatrix} $$

1.2 Matrix with Parentheses (\begin{pmatrix} ... \end{pmatrix})

LaTeX Code:

\[
B = \begin{pmatrix} 
    1 & 2 \\
    3 & 4 
\end{pmatrix}
\]

Rendered Output:

$$ B = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix} $$

1.3 Matrix with Curly Brackets (\begin{Bmatrix} ... \end{Bmatrix})

LaTeX Code:

\[
C = \begin{Bmatrix} 
    1 & 2 \\
    3 & 4 \\
    5 & 6
\end{Bmatrix}
\]

Rendered Output:

$$ C = \begin{Bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \\ 5 & 6 \end{Bmatrix} $$

1.4 Matrix with Vertical Bars (\begin{vmatrix} ... \end{vmatrix})

LaTeX Code:

\[
D = \begin{vmatrix} 
    1 & 2 \\
    3 & 4 
\end{vmatrix}
\]

Rendered Output:

$$ D = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{vmatrix} $$

2. LaTeX Code for Systems of Equations
2.1 System of Linear Equations

LaTeX Code:

\[
\begin{aligned}
    2x + 3y &= 5 \\
    4x - y &= 3
\end{aligned}
\]

Rendered Output:

$$ \begin{aligned} 2x + 3y &= 5 \\ 4x - y &= 3 \end{aligned} $$

2.2 System of Equations in Matrix Form

LaTeX Code:

\[
\begin{bmatrix} 
    2 & 3 \\
    4 & -1 
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix} 
    x \\
    y 
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix} 
    5 \\
    3 
\end{bmatrix}
\]

Rendered Output:

$$ \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 3 \\ 4 & -1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} $$
